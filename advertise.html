For partners

Reach architects
who actually ship.

ArchiGen reaches architects, archviz artists, and design studio leads making real tooling decisions on real projects. Sponsorship is available for AI tool vendors, render hardware, software complements, and adjacent services — under an editorial firewall.

We are picky. We will say no to sponsors whose products we have negatively reviewed. Sponsorship does not buy review coverage, ever.

Email partnerships How we operate

Sponsorship inventory.

Sunday letter primary sponsor (one slot per issue), journal sidebar placements (limited inventory), comparison-page underwriting (clearly disclosed), and sponsored workflow tutorials produced by us under contract.

We do not sell display banners, programmatic ads, or any placement that compromises reading experience. Every sponsorship is reviewed by editorial before sale.

Editorial firewall.

Sponsorship buys placement, not coverage. A sponsored tool gets reviewed exactly the way an unsponsored tool does — same methodology, same critique, same possibility of a negative review.

Sponsored content is labeled as sponsored, with a disclosure of who paid for it. The editor has refusal rights on any partnership that creates a real or perceived conflict.

In the room
  • Sunday letterOne sponsor per issue. ~150 words, custom-written by us. Our reply rate matters more than CTR
  • Journal sidebarLimited inventory across the journal. No takeover units, no autoplay
  • Comparison underwritingSponsor a comparison testbench run. Methodology stays editorial; sponsor is disclosed
  • Custom workflow pieceWe produce a deep tutorial using your tool. Same critical eye as our reviews. Some sponsors decline after seeing the draft. That is the system working
