Pitch us
something.

We commission pieces from practicing architects and archviz artists. If you have shipped a project with AI in the pipeline, run a comparison nobody has written, or have an opinion the field needs to hear — we want to read your pitch.

Pieces are paid. Editing is real. We do not accept finished pieces blind — pitch first.

Send a pitch See the masthead

What we commission.

Field notes (800–1,500 words), essays (2,500–4,000 words), tutorials with reproducible source files, comparison reviews, and short case studies. We commission visuals separately.

We pay practitioner rates: $0.40–$1.00/word depending on length, depth, and exclusivity. Source files and rendered images are paid separately. We pay on acceptance, not on publication.

How to pitch.

Email pitches@archigenai.com with: a one-paragraph thesis, why you are the person to write it, two or three of your prior pieces or projects, and the deadline you can hit. No need for a polished proposal.

We respond to every pitch, usually within seven days. Pitches that fit the editorial line get a response with scope, rate, and deadline. Pitches that do not fit get a clear no with a reason.
